Things to do:

  • Snorkelling, probably in the resort there will be snorkelling available
  • Naama Bay is also a very nice walk. Beware of Point 2 below!!
  • Sharm Old Market, Soho square are nice places to have a walk
  • On Friday and Saturday the weather will be sunny(hopefully) so don't miss the scuba diving trip, don't worry if you didn't do it before, they will help you and it will be safe. seeing the coral reefs in Sharm el-Sheikh is magic! Ras Mohamed or Tiran Island
  • Don't miss Farsha Cafe, it's my personal favorite, it's at El Hadaba, a very cool oriental cafe on the mountain with an amazing view of the sea.
  • Luxor Restaurant in Soho Square for authentic Egyptian dinner and live show
  • Alf Leyla w Leyla for oriental show
  • Turkish Bath
  • Safari trip with buggies or Motos
  • Buy souvenirs from Old Market

Beware!
Please don't get annoyed from the people, just have in mind these points:

  1. People are a bit greedy! so to be safe just do a little bit of research about how much the thing should be (for example if the trip is 100 EGP it's ok to pay 130 or 150 for it but not 300) you can always negotiate :)
  2. Egyptians that work in Sharm el-Sheikh they are a bit sticky! they will be super insisting on you to buy or to eat in their place (Especially in Naama Bay), don't get embarrassed to buy or sit in a place you don't like, just keep walking and say thank you and don't feel bad ignoring them if they are too much ;)
  3. Egyptians also stare a lot so don't worry about this, just don't make eye contact with anyone and pretend no one is looking at you.
  4. Martina please try to stick all the time with the parents and do not go anywhere with anyone!
If there is someone who is really annoying you can mention "tourist police" and they will get afraid :)
